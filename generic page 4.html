“If you live to be a hundred, I want to live to be a hundred minus one day so I never have to live without you”

-A. A. Milne

For your birthday i had something special planned for you but because i certain compnay didn't work with me as good as it does with others plus other complications too my gift didn't make it to you at all but i wil get it to you one day and when it does get to you i hope that you will take lots of pictures of you and the teddy bear

I had a speech and or a letter that i was going to recite to you on the day of your birthday but since it was on a school day and as soon as you were home we didn't really have time to talk i had to keep my little cringy and hopeless romantic letter to myself. Don't worry you're not missing out on a lot

All i will say though is i am glad that you were born and you've grown into a wonderful woman. i am proud of the decisions you have made and the progress you have made so far this year, i know you wil be capable of doing some much more in the future

What you have done right now at the first quarter of the year is enough you have done really well for yourself and i can not be any prouder than i am to be yours and to watch this cute little babe grow to somebody she wanted to be

Their will be more struggles to face in the future but i will be there for each and every single one of them. I will be there for every celebration to come and to be made. You won't spend those special moments alone and if there aren't many or any then i will make more of them for you
